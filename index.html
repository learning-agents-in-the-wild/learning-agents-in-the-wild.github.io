Risk & Safety in Agentic AI

üìç Seoul, South Korea ¬∑ July 2026
Exploring safety challenges and risk mitigation for agentic AI systems

About

Agentic AI systems capable of long-horizon autonomy and real-world interaction are becoming increasingly powerful and widely deployed, raising safety concerns. This workshop provides a forum for researchers and practitioners to study failure modes, risks, and approaches for improving the safety and robustness of agentic AI systems.

Topics

  • Risk assessment and failure analysis for agentic AI systems
  • Red-teaming and adversarial evaluation of agentic behavior
  • Robustness and generalization under distribution shift
  • Safety challenges in multi-agent systems and coordination
  • Guardrails and safety mechanisms for agentic systems
  • Governance, oversight, and policy considerations for agentic AI
  • Case studies of real-world deployment such as web agents and workflow automation
